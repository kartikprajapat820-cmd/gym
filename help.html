Gym Rules & Guidelines

  • ❌ No Outdoor Shoes: Training floor par sirf saaf, separate sports shoes ya gym shoes hi allowed hain. Ghar se vahi shoes pehan kar na aayein.
  • 🏋️‍♂️ Rerack Weights: Dumbbells ya weight plates use karne ke baad unhe unki sahi jagah (rack) par wapas rakhna har member ki zimmedari hai.
  • 🧼 Hygiene First: Workout karte samay hamesha apna personal towel sath rakhein aur cardio machines ko use karne ke baad wipe zaroori karein.
  • 🚫 Respect Space: Dusre members ke set ke beech me bina pooche space block na karein. Mobile ka zyada use floor par avoid karein.
